Ayurveda Services (OPD & IPD)

Ref: DMER AYUSH

Outpatient (OPD) — Ayurveda

Consultations and therapies without admission, delivered by expert Vaidyas.

  • Registration & follow-up desk
  • Ayurvedic consultations: history, examination, diagnosis
  • Specialty clinics: Kayachikitsa, Shalya, Shalakya, Balroga
  • Therapies: Abhyanga, Swedana, Nasya, Basti & more
  • Pharmacy & diagnostic support

Inpatient (IPD) — Ayurveda

Continuous therapies with admission and full clinical monitoring.

  • Admission under senior physician guidance
  • Panchakarma: Vaman, Virechan, Basti, Nasya & more
  • 24/7 supervision by doctors & nursing staff
  • Customised diet & lifestyle guidance
  • Structured discharge & follow-up plan
