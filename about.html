Mission & Vision

Our Direction

Vision

To establish a dedicated hospital that is being built to provide treatment with equality, dignity, and spiritual peace to every patient. This hospital is the dream of Acharyashree Chandprabhsagar Maharaj Ji—where healing is not only physical but also mental and spiritual.

Mission

  • To provide treatment to all patients without any discrimination based on religion, caste, financial status, or social background.
  • To offer affordable and reasonable healthcare services for common people, making it more economical compared to other hospitals in India.
  • To provide special facilities for Tyagi and Pratimadhari individuals, ensuring their religious disciplines are strictly followed, so that they receive mental peace and spiritual support.
  • To provide pure vegetarian food (Shuddha Bhojan) within the hospital.
  • To establish a small temple within the hospital premises, where Tyagi and Pratimadhari individuals can receive mental peace and spiritual support.
